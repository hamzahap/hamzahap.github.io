Modern line capture

Draw the final edge, claim the cell, and chain turns when you close shapes. The game keeps the classic tension but presents it with a cleaner mobile-first feel.

Multiple board families

Square, triangle, hex, diamond, and crown map layouts keep matches fresh without changing the core rules.

Local and solo play

Play 2 to 8 players on one device or challenge Pulse AI in a fast one-on-one match.

What players get

  • Fast path from title screen to match.
  • Touch-friendly boards, haptics, and sound controls.
  • Bright player identity and readable score flow.
  • Ads on menu and result surfaces, not on the live board.
  • One-time Ad-Free unlock for players who want a cleaner loop.

Ads and privacy

EdgeBloom uses Google Mobile Ads and Google User Messaging Platform for ad delivery and consent handling.

  • UMP consent is handled in-app before ads are requested.
  • A privacy choices re-entry point is available when required.
  • The public support files for Play and AdMob live on this domain, including the privacy policy and app-ads.txt.
