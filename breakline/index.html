Precision-first loop

Breakline is built around single-tap timing, fast restarts, and a fixed-speed runner rhythm that makes deaths feel immediate instead of random.

Long-form progression

The campaign spans 20 swipeable chapters and 600 levels with a rising difficulty curve, chapter-specific trap focus, and unlock progression across the route.

Ad-light gameplay

Live gameplay stays free of banner ads. Banner placements are kept to non-gameplay surfaces, rewarded ads are player-triggered, and a one-time remove-ads unlock is available.

What players get

  • One-touch precision play with late landing jump buffering.
  • Manual or auto-retry flow tuned for fast practice loops.
  • Separate settings for sound, lobby music, haptics, and retry behavior.
  • Rewarded continue flow with a post-ad countdown before revive.
  • Chapter and level browsing designed for mobile progression.

Ads and privacy

Breakline uses Google Mobile Ads and Google User Messaging Platform for ad delivery, consent handling, and privacy options where required.

  • UMP consent is checked before the app requests ads.
  • Rewarded ads are only used for the optional Second Wind continue.
  • Interstitial ads are paced, not shown after every level.
  • This shared domain hosts the privacy policy and root app-ads.txt file for AdMob verification.
