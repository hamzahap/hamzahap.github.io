Breakline privacy policy

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Breakline handles information when you use the app on Android.

Back to Breakline Contact support
Effective date: April 7, 2026

1. Who this policy applies to

This policy applies to the Breakline mobile game and the related public support pages for the app.

2. Information stored locally on your device

Breakline keeps core progression and settings local to the device.

  • Campaign progression, best progress, attempts, and unlocks.
  • Selected level or chapter state.
  • Audio, lobby music, haptics, and auto-retry settings.
  • Whether the one-time remove-ads purchase has been restored on the device.

3. Advertising and consent

Breakline may show ads on non-gameplay screens such as menus, post-game surfaces, and other non-live gameplay states. Rewarded ads may also be offered when you choose to use a Second Wind continue.

  • Ads and consent handling are provided through Google services, including Google Mobile Ads and Google User Messaging Platform (UMP).
  • Depending on your region, the app may request consent before ads are loaded or shown.
  • If privacy options are required for your consent flow, the app provides an in-app entry point to review those options again later.
  • The shared developer website for this app also hosts the root app-ads.txt file used for AdMob authorization.

4. In-app purchases

Breakline offers a one-time non-consumable purchase to remove banner and interstitial ads. Purchase processing, billing, and restoration are handled by Google Play.

5. Third-party services

When these services are used, third-party providers may process device identifiers, app activity, consent state, purchase metadata, diagnostics, IP address, or ad performance data according to their own policies.

  • Google Mobile Ads for ad delivery.
  • Google User Messaging Platform for privacy messaging and consent.
  • Google Play Billing for purchases and restore flows.

6. How information is used

  • To run the app and save local progression and settings.
  • To deliver, suppress, or personalize ads based on consent status.
  • To process and restore the remove-ads purchase.
  • To support service diagnostics, fraud prevention, and compliance.

7. Data retention

Local app data remains on your device until it is cleared or the app is uninstalled. Third-party providers retain data according to their own legal, operational, and policy requirements.

8. Your choices

  • You can uninstall the app at any time.
  • You can review in-app privacy options when that entry point is made available for your consent state.
  • You can choose whether to use rewarded Second Wind continues.
  • You can purchase the remove-ads unlock to suppress banner and interstitial ads on the device.

9. Children

Breakline does not intentionally collect personal information through in-app account systems. If the app is distributed in a way that requires special treatment for children or under-age users, ad requests and consent settings can be configured accordingly.

10. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated to reflect product changes, legal requirements, or provider changes. The effective date at the top of this page will be updated when material revisions are made.
